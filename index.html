About Me

Maria Garman

My name is Maria Garman and I am a senior at Dartmouth College. I will graduate in June 2019 with a Major in Engineering Modified with Studio Art and a minor in Human Centered Design. Growing up in Scituate MA, a lovely costal town just south of Boston, an appreciation for the beauty of nature and the colors it creates is engrained in me. My artwork is inspired by the color relationships, patterns, and textures nature creates in these beautiful moments.
